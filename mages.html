WHAT ARE MAGES?

Mage gods in smite are typically characters that have ranged attacks and use magical power to boost the strength of their basic attacks and abilites (though there are some melee mages like Freya). Their main source of damage tends to be their strong god abilities, usually being capable of dealing large amounts of damage in a short period of time, while lacking the ability to withstand damage from the enemy team. Because of this mages are usually not played on the front lines and sit comfortably behind ally tanks that take the damage for them.
