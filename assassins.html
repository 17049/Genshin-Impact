WHAT ARE ASSASSINS?

Assassin gods in smite are typically characters that are melee and use physical power to boost their basic attacks and abilities. They are usually gods who have high mobility and burst damage, being able to go and take down the target before the enemy team can react. Most assassins are able to jungle quite well.
