WHAT IS COOL WEBSITE?

Cool Website is a website that's purpose is to help Smite players learn and awaken their true potential while gaming. Cool Website allows you to view items builds created by other Smite players or create your own for other players to view. Cool Website also has all the simplifed item desciptions for players who want to understand them better.

WHAT IS SMITE?

Smite is a PVP game where you play as either a God or a legend from different Pantheons such as Greek, Norse, or Celtic. Each playable God has different abilities that make them unique in their own ways and that make them exceptional in certain fights.

A lot of Gods are very flexible with playstyles and can be built differently depending on which items you purchase.
