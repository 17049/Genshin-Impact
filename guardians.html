WHAT ARE GUARDIANS?

Guardian gods in smite are typically characters that are melee ranged and use magical power to boost the strength of their basic attacks and abilities. Their damage output tends to be low, but they usually have high crowd control and are capable of withstanding more damage than other classes (eg. Assassins).This allows them to "tank" damage from the others on their team which creates ample opportunites for your allied team to attack.
